Publications

As of today, I have only one publication in a book called Greed in Our Everday World (forthcoming). My chapter, "Greed and Addiction," surveys the conceptual and practical similarities between greed and addiction, and I argue that the differences between common real-life cases turn out to be rather trivial.

In progress

I'm currently working on a literature review that compiles some of the most recent philosophical and empirical research on connections between social media, news consumption, and politics. Eventually, I'd like to turn this into an article arguing that we have an epistemic obligation to limit debating politics on and consuming news from social media.
